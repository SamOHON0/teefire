Our Services

One point of contact for all fire safety needs

We supply, install, commission and maintain every system we work on. No subcontractors, no finger-pointing.

Fire Alarm Systems

Full design, supply and installation of addressable and conventional fire alarm systems. From single-zone panels to large networked installations across hospitals and airports.

Commissioning

Independent commissioning of fire alarm and detection systems to IS 3218 and BS 5839. Full certification and documentation for every project.

Maintenance & Servicing

Scheduled maintenance contracts to keep your systems compliant and operational. Quarterly, bi-annual and annual service plans available nationwide.

Emergency Lighting

Design, installation and testing of emergency lighting systems in compliance with IS 3217. Maintained and non-maintained units available for all building types.

Suppression Systems

Specialist fire suppression for server rooms, kitchens, and high-risk industrial environments. Gas, foam and water mist options tailored to your requirements.

Fire Extinguishers

Supply, installation and annual servicing of all classes of portable fire extinguishers. Compliant with IS 291 and BS 5306 standards.
